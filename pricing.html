Pricing FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through the app settings or by contacting support. No cancellation fees or complex processes.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your recipes remain yours forever. After cancellation, you'll be moved to the free plan (50 recipe limit) and can still access all your data locally.

Do you offer student discounts?

We're working on student and family discounts! Contact us at support@recipeez.app and we'll let you know when they're available.

Is the free trial really free?

Absolutely! No credit card required. Try all premium features for 14 days, then decide if you want to subscribe. No surprises or hidden charges.

How does billing work?

Billing is handled through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). You can manage your subscription through your device's subscription settings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all payment methods supported by the App Store and Google Play, including credit cards, PayPal, and gift cards.
