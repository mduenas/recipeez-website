Frequently Asked Questions

Importing recipes is easy! Simply:

  1. Copy the URL of the recipe page
  2. Open Recipeez and tap the "+" button
  3. Select "Import from URL"
  4. Paste the URL and tap "Import"
  5. Review and edit the imported recipe as needed

Our smart extraction works with most popular recipe websites including AllRecipes, Food Network, Bon App√©tit, and many more.

We believe in privacy-first design. Here's why we use anonymous authentication:

  • Instant Access: Start using the app immediately without forms
  • Complete Privacy: No way to link your recipes to your identity
  • Less Spam: No email means no promotional emails (unless you want them)
  • Data Security: Less personal data means less risk if there's ever a breach
  • Focus on Cooking: Spend time on recipes, not account management

Your data is still secure and backed up using your anonymous device ID.

Your recipes are automatically backed up to the cloud with your premium subscription. Here's how backup works:

  • Automatic Sync: All recipes and photos sync to secure cloud storage
  • Multiple Devices: Access your recipes on any device you sign into
  • Offline Access: Recipes cached locally for offline use
  • Manual Export: Data export feature coming soon for additional backup

Free tier stores data locally on your device only.

Recipe sharing features are in development! Here's what's planned:

  • Individual Sharing: Share specific recipes via link
  • Recipe Collections: Create and share themed collections
  • Community Recipes: Browse recipes shared by other users
  • Family Sharing: Share with family members privately

For now, you can manually copy and send recipe text to others.
