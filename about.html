About Recipeez

The story behind the privacy-first recipe app that puts you in control of your culinary creations.

Our Story

Recipeez was born from a simple frustration: every recipe app wanted too much personal information, tried to do too many things, and didn't respect the fact that your recipes are yours.

We believe cooking is personal. Your grandmother's secret recipe, your late-night kitchen experiments, your family's holiday traditions ‚Äì these aren't just ingredients and instructions. They're memories, creativity, and personal history.

That's why we built Recipeez differently. No email signup. No social media integration. No data mining. Just a beautiful, powerful app that helps you collect, organize, and enjoy your recipes while keeping them completely private.

Our Mission

To create the most beautiful and private recipe management experience possible, where your culinary creativity belongs to you and stays with you forever.

Our Philosophy on Data

In an age where apps collect every detail about your life, we've chosen a different path. We believe:

  • Your recipes are your intellectual property. We never claim ownership or rights to your content.
  • Privacy shouldn't be a premium feature. Anonymous authentication is available to all users, free and paid.
  • Simplicity leads to better security. The less personal data we collect, the less there is to protect or potentially breach.
  • Transparency builds trust. Our privacy policy is written in plain English and explains exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Why We're Different

Most recipe apps are built around advertising, data collection, or social features. We're built around you ‚Äì the home cook who just wants a beautiful, private space to store and organize recipes.

We make money through our subscription model, not by selling your data. This alignment means we're incentivized to build features you'll love and use, not features that extract more data for advertisers.

Looking Forward

We're just getting started. Our roadmap is driven by feedback from real users who cook real meals for real families. Every feature we add is designed to make your cooking life easier while maintaining our commitment to privacy and simplicity.

Thank you for trusting us with your recipes. We take that responsibility seriously and are committed to earning that trust every day.
